# Daltile-Inspired Homepage Redesign

## Overview
Updated the Premier Bathroom homepage to follow the professional, location-focused design pattern of the Daltile Showroom & Design Studio website (https://locations.daltile.com/tx/austin/288/).

## Key Changes Made

### 1. **Hero Section - Showroom Banner**
- **Before**: Gradient background with form and CTA buttons
- **After**: Full-width hero image (IMG_0387.png) with overlay text
- Large, bold headline: "PREMIER BATHROOM SHOWROOM & DESIGN STUDIO"
- Professional tagline: "Austin's Trusted Bathroom Remodeling Experts Since 2008"
- Clean, modern design similar to Daltile's location pages

### 2. **About This Location Section**
- **NEW**: Added dedicated section highlighting the Austin showroom
- Professional narrative describing:
  - Showroom features and product selection
  - Services for homeowners, designers, and contractors
  - Free design consultation with 50+ years combined expertise
  - Commitment to building lasting customer relationships
- Similar to Daltile's "About this Location" section

### 3. **Directions & Contact Section**
- **NEW**: Two-column layout matching Daltile's professional approach
- **Left Column**: 
  - Embedded Google Maps iframe for easy directions
  - Address and location info
  - "Call" and "Schedule Appointment" CTAs
- **Right Column**:
  - Detailed business hours (Mon-Fri 8am-6pm, Sat 9am-3pm, Sun Closed)
  - Quick contact information (phone & email)
  - Professional blue card design

### 4. **What's Next Section**
- **Redesigned**: Four-card grid layout (similar to Daltile's next steps)
- Cards feature:
  - "Browse Services" - links to services page
  - "Get Inspired" - links to project gallery
  - "Free Consultation" - links to contact form
  - "About Us" - links to company story
- Each card has:
  - High-quality bathroom photo
  - Descriptive heading and text
  - Clear call-to-action link

### 5. **Featured Services Section**
- **Enhanced**: Cleaner, more professional service cards
- Three-column grid with:
  - Large service images with hover effects
  - Icon badges for each service
  - Feature bullet points
  - "Learn More" links
- Services highlighted:
  - Bathroom Remodeling
  - Walk-in Bath Installation
  - Shower Remodeling

### 6. **Overall Design Philosophy**
- **Cleaner, More Corporate Look**: Similar to Daltile's professional brand
- **Location-Focused**: Emphasis on Austin showroom and local presence
- **Trust & Credibility**: Business hours, contact info prominently displayed
- **Clear Navigation Path**: "What's Next" guides users to next steps
- **Professional Typography**: Larger, bolder headlines with better hierarchy

## Design Patterns Adopted from Daltile

1. ✅ **Location page structure** - Hero → About → Directions & Contact → What's Next
2. ✅ **Business hours prominently displayed** - Complete weekly schedule
3. ✅ **Google Maps integration** - Embedded iframe for easy navigation
4. ✅ **Professional showroom language** - "Showroom & Design Studio" positioning
5. ✅ **Next steps guidance** - Clear CTA cards for user journey
6. ✅ **Clean white backgrounds** - Professional, uncluttered design
7. ✅ **Trust indicators** - Free consultation, experience, local presence
